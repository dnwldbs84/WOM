Server

How to play

● Collect various magic spells and grow up character, become the greatest mage.

● You can get resources[spell, gold, jewel] and exp from chests, trees, rocks or other characters.

● Use golds and jewels for spell upgrade.

Key Info

● Left-Mouse-click : move character

● E or Space : use character base spell

● 1, 2, 3, 4 : use equiped spell

● A : use character base spell to select area

● S : stop character

● G : open Spell Setting popup

● Enter : chatting

Game Tip

● Using Google Chrome is highly recommended for best performance

● Each Characters Passive Spell make character special.[upgrade it!!!]

● All spells can equip regardless of character type.[except character spell]

● Unlock spell`s property by upgrade.

● If you hit trees or rocks, you will get exp.

● If you destroy chests or kill other character, you will get exp and golds.

● Chests may drop spell and gold and jewel.

● Trees may drop spell and gold and jewel.[low rate]

● Rocks may drop golds and jewel.[low rate]

● Charater may drop spell and gold and jewel.

● If you die, your character will lost resources and even may lost spell level.

● Fire spell can ignite oppenent.

● Ice spell can chill oppenent.

● Arcane spell burn enemy`s MP.[A certain percentage of damage]

● Move speed has max limit.[Base Speed : 170, Max Speed : 300]
